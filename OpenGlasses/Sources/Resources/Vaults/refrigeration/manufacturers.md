# Manufacturer Overview

## Carrier

Commercial chiller and rooftop unit lines commonly encountered in field service.

- **30RB / 30RAP / 30XA** — Air-cooled scroll/screw chillers. Common controller: Carrier ComfortLink. Error codes appear on the front display as `Exx` or `Txx`.
- **WeatherMaker rooftop units** (48/50 series) — Light commercial rooftops. ComfortLink controller. Error codes `T01`–`T26` for sensor and refrigerant circuit faults.
- **Aquasnap (30RA/30RAP)** — Outdoor air-cooled. Pre-2010 units may have legacy controller without alphanumeric display.

Carrier uses R-410A in most post-2010 small/medium commercial systems. New deployments may use R-32 or R-454B. Confirm refrigerant from nameplate before service.

## Trane

- **Voyager rooftop series** — Common DX rooftop. Reliatel or Symbio controller. Trane "Active Alarm" displays as `A`-prefixed codes (`A100`, `A200` ranges).
- **Intellipak** (commercial rooftop) — Symbio 800 controller. Touchscreen interface.
- **CGAM / CGAF / RTAC** — Air-cooled scroll chillers. Tracer SC+ or Symbio controller.

Trane systems frequently use R-410A. R-454B appearing in new Voyager units from 2024 onward.

## Daikin

- **Rebel / Rebel Applied** — Light commercial rooftop. Often R-32 in newer builds.
- **VRV IV / VRV 5** — Variable refrigerant volume systems. Daikin proprietary controllers. R-410A traditionally; newer VRV 5 uses R-32.
- **Maverick II rooftop** — Symbio 800 (same controller line as Trane).

VRV systems are highly proprietary — always have manufacturer service tool available.

## Lennox

- **Energence rooftop units** — Light commercial. M3 / Prodigy controller. Codes display as `E_xx` or `A_xx`.
- **Strata rooftop chillers** — Larger commercial. Newer units use Lennox iComfort controller.

R-410A is dominant; R-454B introduced 2024 in some Energence units.

## Mitsubishi Electric

- **Mr. Slim / City Multi VRF** — Light commercial split / VRF. Codes are 4-character alphanumeric (e.g. `P8`, `U1`, `FB`).
- **Mr. Slim P-Series** — Larger ductless commercial.

R-410A standard. R-32 in newer City Multi G generation.
